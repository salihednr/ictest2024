ICTEST-2024

The ICTEST-2024 conference covers all aspects of intelligent and smart computing systems design, including software and hardware. IEEE Kerala Section is a technical co-sponsor of the conference. Attendees can learn about current research and development in Information Technology at ICTEST-2024. In addition to Intelligent Systems and Applications, Big Data Processing, Internet of Things and Blockchain, Secure Information Processing, Cloud Computing, Edge and Fog Computing, Robotics and automation, Scalable Computing, Neuromorphic Engineering, Multimedia Systems, Electronic Design Automation, Emerging Technologies, Healthcare systems, Power and Energy Systems, and more, ICTEST'24 covers a wide range of topics. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.

Partners

Indexing Partners

One of the prominent indexing partners of IEEE is the IEEE Xplore Digital Library. This comprehensive online repository is a premier resource for accessing IEEE's journals, conference proceedings, standards, and technical papers. IEEE Xplore offers advanced search capabilities, enabling users to explore and retrieve relevant content from a vast collection of scholarly articles.
Additionally, IEEE collaborates with indexing partners such as Scopus, Web of Science (Clarivate Analytics), Google Scholar, and PubMed. These indexing services provide broad coverage across multiple disciplines, allowing researchers to discover and cite IEEE publications in their respective fields of study. Inclusion in these prestigious indexes enhances the visibility, credibility, and impact of IEEE research.

Publication Partners

IEEE, the prestigious organization dedicated to advancing technology, collaborates with a wide range of publication partners to disseminate its scholarly publications. Prominent publication partners of IEEE include Springer, Wiley, Elsevier, Taylor & Francis, and the ACM Digital Library. These partnerships allow IEEE to extend the reach of its research output and make it accessible to a broader audience. IEEE works closely with these publishers to ensure the highest standards of quality and integrity in its publications. Through its publication partners, IEEE journals, conference proceedings, and other scholarly content gain greater visibility and impact in the scientific community. These collaborations also facilitate interdisciplinary research collaborations and knowledge exchange among researchers and practitioners. Publication partners play a crucial role in archiving and preserving the valuable research presented at IEEE conferences, making it accessible to researchers for future reference. By partnering with renowned publishers, IEEE ensures that its publications are widely available through various academic platforms and libraries. These partnerships enable researchers, academics, and professionals from around the world to access and cite IEEE's cutting-edge research findings. Overall, the publication partners of IEEE contribute significantly to the organization's mission of advancing technology and promoting global innovation through the dissemination of high- quality scholarly publications.
