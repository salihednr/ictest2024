FAQ

Here are some of the frequently asked questions

Q.
A.
Who Can Participate ?
1. Poster authors must be undergraduate students at the time of submission and may be members of a team comprising of up to four students.
2. At least one student author must be an IEEE student member at the time of the submission of the poster to the Contest.
3. The poster should be fully the work of the student(s) and completed before they have received their respective engineering or scientific degree.
Q.
A.
How To Submit ?
To participate in the Competition students should write a 400-500-word description (abstract) of the poster, along with author’s names, respective university and contact details; and submit in PDF in mail id ictest24@mec.ac.in
The abstracts will be reviewed and authors notified about the acceptance of their poster by Feb 15 th , 2024.
If you have any questions or problems submitting your abstract, please contact: in ictest24@mec.ac.in
Q.
A.
What Is The Deadline To Submit The Abstract Idea Of Poster ?
The deadline to submit poster abstracts is midnight December 31 st , 2024.
Q.
A.
Is There Any Poster Specifications ?
Yes, Posters must be in PDF no larger than 36”x48” if printed and no larger than 10 MB
Q.
A.
Is There Any Registration Fees ?
Yes, After the poster is accepted, contestants must register through email. Registration is not required to submit an abstract. (Poster registration is separate from ICTEST’24 and does not cover attendance at ICTEST’24.)
Registration fee is Rs. 2000 per poster (at least one student has to be an IEEE student member).
Additional students may participate as the audience for no additional fee.

ICTEST-2024

The ICTEST-2024 conference covers all aspects of intelligent and smart computing systems design, including software and hardware. IEEE Kerala Section is a technical co-sponsor of the conference. Attendees can learn about current research and development in Information Technology at ICTEST-2024. In addition to Intelligent Systems and Applications, Big Data Processing, Internet of Things and Blockchain, Secure Information Processing, Cloud Computing, Edge and Fog Computing, Robotics and automation, Scalable Computing, Neuromorphic Engineering, Multimedia Systems, Electronic Design Automation, Emerging Technologies, Healthcare systems, Power and Energy Systems, and more, ICTEST'24 covers a wide range of topics. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements.
